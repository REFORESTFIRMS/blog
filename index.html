👤 About Me

🚀 Brand Strategy Consultant | Sales Leader | Digital Growth Strategist

I'm a results-driven consultant with 15+ years of experience in sales leadership, market expansion, and brand development. I’ve helped businesses—from traditional FMCG players to emerging D2C brands—build their market presence with strategic clarity and executional discipline.

My journey began in the trenches of the building materials sector as a Sales Officer, where I honed my instincts on customer psychology, positioning, and operational impact. Over time, I rose to lead national teams as General Manager, driving revenue, launching new brands, and scaling go-to-market strategies.

Today, I work with FMCG, Retail, and D2C startups as a freelance brand strategist—helping them craft brand stories, fine-tune messaging, and build marketing systems that sell. Every project I take is rooted in real-world insight, growth focus, and long-term thinking.
